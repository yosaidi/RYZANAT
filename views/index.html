The juniper tree is a symbol of resilience and natural beauty. Known for its evergreen foliage and aromatic wood, it thrives in diverse climates.

The raffia palm is renowned for its long, durable leaves, which are traditionally harvested to create natural fibers. These fibers are widely used in crafting textiles, baskets, and ropes, making the raffia palm a symbol of sustainable artistry and resourcefulness.

The Moroccan zarbia, or traditional rug, is a masterpiece of craftsmanship and cultural heritage. Handwoven by skilled artisans, these rugs feature intricate patterns, vibrant colors, and a blend of wool and natural fibers. Each zarbia tells a unique story, reflecting Morocco's rich history and artistic traditions.
